### Card Sizes
- **Small:** 1x1 grid units
- **Medium:** 2x1 or 1x2 grid units
- **Large:** 2x2 grid units

### Design Principles
1. **Important First:** Most critical info in large tiles
2. **Visual Hierarchy:** Size indicates importance
3. **White Space:** Breathing room between cards
4. **Consistent Padding:** 16px between cards
5. **Tap Targets:** Minimum 48x48dp for touch areas
